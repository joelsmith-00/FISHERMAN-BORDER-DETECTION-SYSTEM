Hardware Layer

Hardware: The Eyes and Ears at Sea

Boat Mounted Edge-AI Device

  • 🛰️GPS & IMU Sensors: Continuously track boat location, speed, and direction.
  • 📡LoRa Communication Module: Creates a long-range boat-to-boat mesh network that works even when cellular signal is unavailable.
  • 🔊Voice Alert Interface: High-power speakers provide real-time Tamil voice warnings so fishermen do not need to look at a screen.
  • 🌊Environmental Sensors: Monitor atmospheric pressure and sea conditions.

Wearable Safety Band

  • 🔗BLE Connectivity: Connected to the boat device via Bluetooth Low Energy.
  • 📈Motion Sensors: Accelerometer and gyroscope detect sudden falls.
  • 🆘Man-Overboard Detection: Detects fall patterns using sensor fusion.
  • 🚨Emergency SOS Button: Manual panic trigger that alerts the boat system instantly.

System Architecture Pipeline

System Data Flow

How Marine Guardian AI processes information from sensors to emergency response.

Step 1 📍

Sensor Data Collection

Boat sensors collect real-time GPS, IMU motion data, and atmospheric pressure.

Step 2 🧠

Edge AI Processing

The onboard device processes sensor data locally without needing internet connectivity.

Step 3 📈

Risk Prediction Engine

AI models detect border drift risk, dangerous sea conditions, and fall incidents.

Step 4 🔊

Voice Alert System

Fishermen receive real-time Tamil voice warnings without needing to check screens.

Step 5 📶

LoRa Mesh Communication

Emergency signals are relayed between nearby boats even when there is no cellular signal.

Step 6 🏖️

Shore Hub Gateway

The shore station collects offshore data and prepares it for cloud synchronization.

Step 7 ☁️

AWS Cloud Processing

Authorities receive alerts while historical trip data is stored for analysis.
